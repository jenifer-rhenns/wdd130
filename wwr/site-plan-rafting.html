Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to ensure that more and more people have the opportunity to venture into clear water currents and enjoy contact with nature safely, fun and respectfully.

Audience

Who are the target customers? People who love nature, and adventures, people who are looking for some different hobbies and experiences, to collect new experiences and moments with adrenaline and satisfaction by rafting with other people as a group. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They want to know more about the company, the offered activities, the prices, and why they can trust us. What are their needs that aren't being met? A company that inspires a love for what does with security and responsibility. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? mobile device and laptop.
