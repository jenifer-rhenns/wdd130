RaftLife

Our Purpose

is to ensure that more people have the opportunity to venture into clear water currents and enjoy contact with nature in a safe, fun, and respectful manner.

History

RaftLife, founded in 1985 by adventurous siblings Jenifer and Charlene Rhenns, began as a small family business offering white water rafting tours on the roaring rivers of Colorado. With a deep passion for the outdoors and a commitment to safety and environmental stewardship, RaftLife quickly gained a reputation for providing exhilarating and memorable experiences. Over the years,

the company expanded its operations, adding new locations across the United States and incorporating a variety of adventure packages. Today, RaftLife is a leading name in the white water rafting industry, known for its expert guides, top-notch equipment, and dedication to preserving the natural beauty of America's rivers.
